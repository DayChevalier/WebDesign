The Stan Lee Foundation

The Stan Lee Foundation was founded in 2010 and is a non-profit organization that seeks to provide access to literacy, education, and the arts throughout an unspecified nation. Lee has maintained a passion for the literary arts and a desire to share the written word. With the Stan Lee Foundation, that commitment to literacy, arts, and education will be instilled in future generations for years to come.

The foundation hopes to provide innovative, best-of-breed technology resources and services, professional development and digital curriculum to support and improve access to literacy and learner readiness programs, resources and services that leads to higher education. Provide the opportunity to participate and experience the arts. Create literacy-centered partnerships with foundations, businesses, industry, local schools, government and other entities, that support and enhance the global literacy community Create awareness for successful programs in literacy as well as for the pursuit of education and appreciation for the arts that will lead to increased funding for those programs to grow. Support effective educational, developmental and adult basic education programs and services. Support emerging and successful programs and services for the youth in literacy, education and arts that creates interest for them to continue and pursue educational and artistic endeavors. Promote community engagement and giving towards literacy, educational, and artistic pursuits.
