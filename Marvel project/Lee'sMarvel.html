Stan Lee's Marvel

Welcome to the newest fan site dedicated to the king of comics, Stan Lee. Writer, editor, actor, producer, publisher, television personality, and the former president and chairman of Marvel Comics.

A fan of Stan Lee? Then you've come to the right place! Don't know who Stan Lee is? Once again, you've come to the right place! In collaboration with several artists, he co-created Spider-Man, the Hulk, the X-Men, the Fantastic Four, Iron Man, Thor, and many other fictional characters, introducing complex, natrualistic characters and a thoroughly shared universe into superhero comic books. Setting the stage for the graphic novels still produce and read by even today's youth.

This site features a short biography, a list of some of his more popular works, a small piece on the various TV and movie roles he's played, and a light over view of the Stan Lee Foundation. So if you wish to know more about the 'Man behind Marvel' click through a couple of the above links!

We here at 'Stan Lee's Marvel' are in the process of developing an extensive database, to keep record of the thousands of Marvel made comic books that are made even today. This will take time and plenty of day by day updates. So please bear with us as the site is still technically under construction.
